Unit 9 Idioms describing people

Positive and negative qualities

Positive

a heart of gold = very kind, generous

as good as gold = helpful, well-behaved

Negative

as hard as nails = no sympathy for others

a nasty piece fo work = unpleasant

Note also

heart’s in the right place = is good and kind person even though they do not always seem so

an awkward customer = difficult person to deal with

a pain in the neck = nuisance 讨厌的人, difficult

get on everyone’s nerves = irritates everyone

Idioms based on ‘head’

to have your head screwed on (be sensible, informal)

to have a head for heights (not suffer from vertigo 晕眩)

to have a head like a sieve (bad memory)

to have a good head for figures (be good at maths)

your head in the clouds (unaware of reality)

to be head and shoulders above someone (much better than)

to bury your head in the sand (refuse to think about a difficult situation in the hope you won’t have to deal with it)

to keep your head (stay calm in a difficult situation)

How people relate to the social norm

an odd-ball (peculiar 奇怪的，古怪的, strange)

over the top (very exaggerated in behaviour)

round the bend (absolutely crazy/mad)

middle- of- the- road (very normal; no radical ideas; neither left- nor right-wing)

Who’s who in the class? Idioms for people in the classroom

teacher’s pet (teacher’s favourite)

top of the class

a real know-all (thinks he knows everything)

a big-head (has a high opinion of him/herself)

lazy-bones

Unit 8 Describing people - personality

Intellectual ability

lots of ability:

intelligent, bright, clever, sharp, shrewd, able, gifted, talented, brainy(informal)

lacking ability

stupid, foolish, simple, silly, brainless, daft, dumb, dim, thick(informal and offensive)

clever, in a negative way, using brains to trick or deceive

cunning = crafty = sly 狡猾的

Attitudes toward life

pessimistic 悲观的 optimistic 乐观的

extroverted 外向的 introverted 内向的

tense 紧绷的 (wound up/ stressed out) relaxed

sensible 明智的 down-to-earth 务实的

sensitive upset(worked-up)

Attitudes towards other people

爱好交际的：sociable gregarious

不同意他人：quarrelsome argumentative

快乐建立在别人的痛苦上：cruel sadistic

对自己和他人态度随和：easy-going even-tempered laid-back

对他人说实话：honest trustworthy reliable sincere

对他人无礼：impolite rude ill-mannered discourteous

别人有你没有的东西就不开心：jealous envious
